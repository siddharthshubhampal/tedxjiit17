Transformation Gen X

Generation X, the main influencers, architects and drivers of digital transformation, are the Digital Immigrants. With a high level of scepticism, “what’s in it for me?” defines them as the consumers. Generation Y, the Digital Natives have seen the age of technological change, globalization and economic disruption. Generation Z, the digital integrators, having technology integrated seamlessly into their lives, referred to as Neo-Digital Natives.

They are expected to be flexible, tech-savvy, inquisitive, and financially driven. There’s the challenge of the global economy. Even previously “safe” careers such as law and medicine are being disrupted by technology. However, with the increasing prominence of technology and globalization, there are new opportunities that they can pioneer.

Working with individuals from multiple generations may seem like a daunting task because no single structure will keep all three generations satisfied with the job. Here’s the opportunity for you all to get an insight of the hand-on experience from these three generations.

TED

TED -Technology, Entertainment, Design is a non-profit media organization which posts videos of powerful talks online, for free distribution, under the slogan "ideas worth spreading", ideas which are known to change perspectives and provide food for thought. TED was founded in February 1984 as a conference, which has been held annually since 1990. TED's early emphasis was technology and design, but it has since broadened its focus to include talks on many scientific, cultural, and academic topics.

TED is synonymous with world-changing ideas with multiple initiatives. At TED, people who are great in their thoughts and actions, are asked to give the talk of their lives in 18 minutes. Talks are then made available, free, at TED.com. TED speakers have included Bill Gates, Jane Goodall, Elizabeth Gilbert, Sir Richard Branson, Benoit Mandelbrot, Philippe Starck, Ngozi Okonjo-Iweala, Isabel Allende and former UK Prime Minister Gordon Brown. Two major TED events are held each year: The TED Conference takes place every spring in Long Beach, California (along with a parallel conference, TEDActive, in Palm Springs), and TEDGlobal is held each summer in Edinburgh, Scotland.

TED x

TEDx was created in the spirit of TED's mission, "ideas worth spreading." It is an international community that organizes TED-style events anywhere and everywhere -- celebrating locally-driven ideas and elevating them to a global stage. TEDx events are produced independently of TED conferences, each event curates speakers on their own, but based on TED's format and rules. These local, self-organized events are branded TEDx, where x = independently organized TED event. The TEDx Program is designed to help communities, organizations and help spark deep conversation and connections at the local level. TEDx events are planned and coordinated independently, under a free license granted by TED.

An engineer by profession ,a student of astrophysics, and a recognised litterateur. His contributions include his remarkable involvement in the study of black hole binaries and multiple black hole systems, apart from being an academic he is also a poet of varied tastes and genres.

He has also volunteered for and initiated many social-educational ventures for example Right to Information Act, Environment Education, Interlinking of communities for tourism enhancement in Agra, Yamuna Action Plan-II, Gangajal Project etc.

He is seen as one of the many prominent change-bringers in the city of Agra. He majorly contributed in the popularization and communication of sciences by conducting major workshops, lecture series and exhibition reaching to almost 100,000 people in past 7 years. He is also authoring a popular science book on astronomy to be published by March 2016.

Taking into account contributions, he was awarded the REX Karmaveer Global Fellowship and recognition by iCONGO in association with United Nations and Fellowship of the Royal Astronomical Society for his outstanding contribution in popularization of physics & astronomy.

He recently organized Astronomy at Taj-2016 (Winter School on Astronomy & Workshop on Star Formation) which was a confluence of the finest minds in astronomy and astrophysics across the globe to discuss the nature and the fate of the universe.
